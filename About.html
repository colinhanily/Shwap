PartySwap is a fully decentralised protocol for trustless peer to peer ERC20 token swaps.

1. Alice creates a swap with Bob using the PartySwap contract
2. Alice and Bob both deposit there tokens to the contract
4. Once Alice has withdrawn Bobs tokens, she can no longer withdraw her own tokens, leaving them for Bob to withdraw, and vice versa
3. Alice and Bob can now withdraw each others tokens
